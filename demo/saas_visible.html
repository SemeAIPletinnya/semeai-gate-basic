Stop unsupported AI answers before users see them.

A host product sends the user message, AI answer, business data, and rules. SemeAI Gate returns a business action: SHOW, REVIEW, or BLOCK.

1. User message The customer asks the existing chatbot.
2. AI answer The chatbot proposes a candidate answer.
3. Business data Rules and known facts are checked.
4. Gate action SHOW, REVIEW, or BLOCK is returned.
5. Audit The decision can be inspected later.
