Why buyers care

Fluent AI is already in production. Ungoverned release is the risk.

  • 1
    Stop invented promos & refunds Support bots invent codes when business data is empty — Gate blocks them.
  • 2
    Contain finance & eligibility claims No proof in business data → REVIEW or BLOCK, not a confident hallucination.
  • 3
    Keep an audit when you say no Receipts make “we blocked it” provable for compliance and incident review.
